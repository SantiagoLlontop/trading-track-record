## Methodology Notes

- **Net P&L:** gross gaming profit net of losses, before operational expenses and taxes. Reconciled to source ledgers across all 20 months.
- **ROI basis:** measured on rotated working capital (2–3× per month), reflecting capital efficiency, not compounding.
- **Sharpe caveat:** arbitrage is near market-neutral, so traditional Sharpe overstates risk-adjusted return. True risk is operational (account limitation, execution error), not return variance.
- **Max drawdown:** largest peak-to-trough decline on the session-level cumulative equity curve.
